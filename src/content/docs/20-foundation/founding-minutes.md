# Minutes of the Constitutive Assembly

## 1. Meeting Information

A constitutive assembly of the founding members of **Earth Bridge Alliance** was held on **02 July 2026** in the form of a remote meeting (video/teleconference).

All founding members were present and agreed to waive any formal convening requirements.

---

## 2. Attendance

The following founding members participated:

- Jasper Felix Meyer
- Guillaume Ferre

---

## 3. Chair of the Meeting

The assembly was chaired by **Jasper Felix Meyer**, acting as chair of the constitutive meeting.

Guillaume Ferre assisted with drafting the minutes for the purpose of preparing these minutes. This function does not constitute a governance office within the Association.

---

## 4. Agenda

- Formation of the Association Earth Bridge Alliance
- Approval of the Bylaws
- Confirmation of founding members
- Appointment of initial officers
- Authorization of administrative formalities required for incorporation

---

## 5. Resolutions

### Resolution 1 — Formation of the Association

The founding members unanimously approved the creation of the association **Earth Bridge Alliance**, governed by the French Law of 1 July 1901.

---

### Resolution 2 — Adoption of the Bylaws

The founding members unanimously adopted the **Bylaws v0.1.0** of the Association.

---

### Resolution 3 — Founding Members

The assembly confirmed the status of founding members as:

- Jasper Meyer
- Guillaume Ferre

---

### Resolution 4 — Appointment of Officers

The following appointments were unanimously approved:

- **President**: Jasper Meyer  
- **Mandataire (Administrative Delegate)**: Guillaume Ferre  

---

### Resolution 5 — Authorization of Formalities

The assembly unanimously authorized Guillaume Ferre, acting as Mandataire, to carry out all administrative and legal formalities required for the registration and initial establishment of the Association.

---

## 6. Closure of the Meeting

There being no further business, the meeting was closed.

---

## 7. Certification

These minutes certify the resolutions adopted by the founding members during the constitutive assembly of Earth Bridge Alliance.

Executed on 02 July 2026.

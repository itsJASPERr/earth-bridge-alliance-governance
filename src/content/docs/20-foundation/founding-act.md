# Founding Act

## Purpose

This Founding Act constitutes the formal legal act by which the undersigned founding members establish **Earth Bridge Alliance** as a non-profit association governed by the **Loi du 1er juillet 1901** and applicable French legislation.

This document serves as the constitutive legal instrument of the Association and forms part of the **Legal Formation Package v0.1**.

---

# Article 1 — Formation of the Association

The undersigned founding members hereby declare the creation of the association named:

> **Earth Bridge Alliance**

The Association is established as a non-profit organization under the French Law of 1 July 1901 relating to associations.

The Association shall acquire legal personality upon completion of the applicable registration procedures before the competent French authorities.

---

# Article 2 — Adoption of the Bylaws

The founding members unanimously adopt the **Bylaws v0.1.0** as the governing constitution of the Association.

The Bylaws define the governance structure, objectives, membership framework, decision-making procedures, financial principles, and dissolution process of Earth Bridge Alliance.

The adopted Bylaws are incorporated by reference into this Founding Act.

---

# Article 3 — Founding Members

The founding members recorded in the **Founding Members Register v0.1.0** are recognized as the initial members of the Association.

---

# Article 4 — Initial Governance Appointments

The founding members unanimously appoint the initial officers of the Association as follows:

| Function | Appointed Member |
|----------|------------------|
| President | Jasper Meyer |
| Mandataire (Administrative Delegate) | Guillaume Ferre |

The appointed officers accept their respective responsibilities in accordance with the Bylaws and applicable French law.

The **Mandataire** is specifically empowered to carry out the administrative formalities necessary for the legal establishment of the Association.

---

# Article 5 — Registered Office

The registered office of the Association shall be established at the address declared during the registration process.

Any subsequent relocation shall be carried out in accordance with the procedures established in the Bylaws and applicable French law.

---

# Article 6 — Authority Granted

The founding members authorize **Guillaume Ferre**, acting as **Mandataire**, to carry out all administrative and legal formalities necessary for the declaration and registration of the Association before the competent French authorities.

Following the successful registration of the Association, the Mandataire is further authorized to undertake the initial administrative steps required to make the Association operational, including:

- submitting the declaration of the Association;
- filing the adopted Bylaws and supporting documentation;
- communicating with the competent French administrative authorities;
- receiving correspondence relating to the incorporation process;
- applying for any identification numbers or registrations required by law;
- opening and activating the Association's first bank account in the name of **Earth Bridge Alliance**;
- completing any administrative procedures reasonably necessary to enable the Association to commence its activities.

This mandate is limited to the incorporation and initial establishment of the Association and does not modify, restrict, or supersede the governance powers assigned to the President or any other governing body under the Bylaws.

Upon completion of these initial establishment formalities, the Mandataire's delegated authority shall automatically terminate. Thereafter, the governance, legal representation, and administration of the Association shall be exercised in accordance with the Bylaws and applicable French law.

---

# Article 7 — Effective Date

This Founding Act shall enter into legal effect upon signature by all founding members.

The Association shall obtain legal personality upon completion of its registration under the applicable provisions of French law.

---

# Article 8 — Governance Dependencies

This document:

- creates the legal entity **Earth Bridge Alliance**;
- adopts the Bylaws;
- recognizes the Founding Members Register;
- appoints the initial governance officers;
- delegates incorporation and establishment authority to the Mandataire;
- shall subsequently be validated by the Founding Minutes of the Constitutive Assembly.

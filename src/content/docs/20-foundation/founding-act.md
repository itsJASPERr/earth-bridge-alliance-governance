# Founding Act

## Establishment of the Association

On **[Date]**, the undersigned founding members hereby establish an association under the French Law of 1 July 1901 and the Decree of 16 August 1901.

The Association shall bear the name:

**Earth Bridge Alliance**

---

## Purpose

The Association is established to pursue the purposes described in the Bylaws adopted on the same date.

---

## Adoption of the Bylaws

The founding members unanimously approve and adopt the Bylaws of Earth Bridge Alliance as the governing document of the Association.

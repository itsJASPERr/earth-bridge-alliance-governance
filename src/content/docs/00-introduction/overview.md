# Earth Bridge Alliance Governance

Welcome to the governance repository of **Earth Bridge Alliance**.

This repository serves as the single source of truth for the organization's governing documents. It is maintained using version control to ensure transparency, accountability, and a complete historical record of how the organization evolves over time.

## Purpose

The repository provides a structured framework for the governance of Earth Bridge Alliance, including its legal foundation, constitutional documents, operational policies, board decisions, and supporting reference material.

Our objective is to create governance that is:

- Transparent
- Adaptable
- Legally compliant
- Easy to understand
- Sustainable for future leadership

## Repository Structure

The documentation is organized into the following sections:

### 00 – Introduction

Provides an introduction to the governance repository and its development roadmap.

### 10 – Architecture

Describes the philosophy, structure, and long-term vision of the governance system.

### 20 – Foundation

Contains the historical documents establishing the organization. These documents become permanent records once adopted.

### 30 – Charter

Contains the constitutional documents governing Earth Bridge Alliance, including its bylaws.

### 40 – Board

Contains board resolutions and governance decisions adopted by the Board of Directors.

### 50 – Policies

Contains operational policies that guide the day-to-day administration of the organization.

### 60 – Reference

Contains supporting documentation such as glossaries, templates, and change logs.

## Governance Philosophy

Earth Bridge Alliance adopts a principle of **minimum necessary governance**.

Governance should enable the organization to achieve its mission while avoiding unnecessary complexity or administrative burden.

Wherever possible, the organization prefers:

- Principles over procedures
- Policies over bylaws
- Templates over repetition
- Modular documentation over monolithic manuals

## Versioning

All governance documents are maintained using Git.

Every change is version controlled, reviewed, and permanently recorded to preserve the institutional memory of the organization.

## Contributing

Governance evolves together with the organization.

Changes should be proposed through the established review process and approved by the appropriate governing body before becoming effective.
